Waffles Recipe

How to make waffles!

Everybody loves to eat waffles! Perhaps the only thing better than waffles, is more waffles! Below is the best recipe to makking your own homemade waffles. This will contain the ingredients and the steps on how to make these waffles. Go ahead and give it a shot!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 4 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 and a half cups of warm milk
  • One third cups of melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.
  3. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately.
